Easy Tacos

Description

This recipe involves a customizable dish with primarily meat as a protein rolled into a flour tortilla; I particularly enjoy putting mexican cheese and lettuce on my tacos! :)

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 oz.) carton Campbell's Mexican Style Tomato Soup
  • 12 taco shells, warmed
  • 1/3 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • 3/4 cup diced tomato

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in a 12 inch skillet over medium-high heat until the beef is well browned, stirring often to separate meat. Pour off any fat. Season with salt and black pepper.
  2. Stir the soup in the skillet and heat to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cook for 5 minutes or until the soup is reduced, stirring often.
  3. Divide the beef among the taco shells. Top with the lettuce, tomato and cheese.
